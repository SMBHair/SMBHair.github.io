Register
The registration fee per team this year will be $40. Registration will open on October 25, 2022, and close on November 7, 2022 at 11:59 PM EST. Payment will not be due until after final teams are decided. Teams that wish to register past this deadline should contact us through email.
We do not enforce a team cap per school; however, we unfortunately anticipate only being able to allow a maximum of 30 teams to compete in this year’s competition. This means that all registered B or C teams will hold less priority over all registered A teams to promote fairness for smaller schools. Final registrations will be determined by the board of UMASO, factoring into account each team’s previous experience with tournaments, the number of teams in their school, and their shown interest in attendance.
Following registration closing, all students competing on campus must fill out appropriate liability forms due to the in-person nature of the event. Teams must conform to official Science Olympiad 2023 rules regarding Division C membership and eligibility for invitational tournament participation, including the fulfillment of all state fees prior to competition in the UMASO tournament.
Once registration opens, the link to the form will be right here!
