Format
This year’s tournament will be a Division C Science Olympiad Invitational tournament held on February 17, 2024, in-person on the UMass Amherst campus.
Our tournament will run all 23 national Science Olympiad events. Trial events, details about event conflicts, and scheduling will be announced at a future date. We expect to provide individual homerooms for all competing schools.
This year's tournament will also emphasize build events. More detailed information about how our tournament will run will be released soon!
Timeline
  • October 20th: Registration closes
  • October 24th: Team notification and payment begins
  • November 20th: Payment deadline
  • February 17th: In-person tournament date
